About Me

Hello, my name is Moses O. Oyelade a Software Developer passionate about building scalable backend systems, intuitive web applications, and data-driven solutions. I enjoy transforming ideas into efficient digital products using tools like JavaScript/TypeScript, Python, Django, NestJS, and React, while applying clean architecture and modern engineering best practices to deliver reliable and impactful outcomes.

My foundation in Medical Biochemistry gives me a unique analytical mindset blending structured scientific problem-solving with innovative software engineering. Before advancing into full-time development, I gained professional experience as a Site Manager at A & P (Pladis) Foods Ltd under Febdol Corporate Cleaners and later as an IT Officer at Jumbol Engineering, supporting business operations and IT infrastructure.

I have further strengthened my software engineering experience through my work with RescueTap, a technology platform optimizing emergency and medical response workflows. There, I contribute to software development efforts that enhance user experience, improve operational efficiency, and support critical real-world impact especially in health-related environments.

Today, I’m focused on building a strong career in software engineering with particular interest in backend development, health IT, and AI-driven solutions. My goal is to work collaboratively on innovative projects that solve meaningful problems, improve access to services, and deliver lasting value to people and organizations.

I’m continuously learning, improving, and contributing driven by curiosity, resilience, and a passion for technology that makes a difference.
